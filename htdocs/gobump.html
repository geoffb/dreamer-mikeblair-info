From Ghosties and Ghoulies

This page is dedicated to all the things, whether real or imagined, that scare us. Especially late at night, when it is very still, and we are all alone. So I hope you enjoy this journey into all things dark and just a little bit scary.

My first exposure to things of a darker nature came when I was a small child. My mother would read poetry to me at night. Some of my favorites were The Highway Man and Little Orphan Annie. As I grew older, I became more and more fascinated by Vampires, Werewolves, Mummies and all the other creatures of myth and folklore that inhabit the dark recesses of the mind. And then one day I discovered Fantasy Art.
